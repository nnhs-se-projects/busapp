Adding App To Your Android Homescreen


Firstly, go to Chrome, and go to the website, which you should have done already. Your screen should roughly look like this:



Click on the 3 vertical dots that you see in the top right. You will then see this small menu on the top right of your screen:



Click on the option that says Add to home screen. After doing this, you will see this small sub menu:



Almost there... Just one step left!!! Finally, click Add. See? Nice and simple. If you now navigate to the homescreen of your Android device, you will see an icon. Hopefully, you found this guide useful.
